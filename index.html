Different Types of Crypto Currency

Dogecoin (DODGE)
Originally created as a joke inspired by Bitcoin, it was marketed because of its meme featuring the Shiba Inu dog. But this crypotcurrency offers unlimited issuance and may be used for payments or/and sending money.
price: 0.1711 USD
market cap: 25.08 billion USD
BNB (BNB)
A cryptocurrency issued by Binance, one of the largest crypto exchanges in the world. Originally created as a token to pay for discounted trades, it can be used for payments, purchasing various goods and services.
price: 609.02 USD
market cap: 88.89 billion USD
Solana (SOL)
Launched last March 2020, Solana is a newer cryptocurrency than the rest. It touts is speed at completing transactions and overall robustness of its "web-scale" platform. It has capped 480 million coins.
price: 179.94 USD
market cap: 84.69 billion USD
